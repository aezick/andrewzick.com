College Basketball


March Madness is possibly the most phenominally unpredictable sporting event in the United States, if not the world. "The thrill of victory...and the agony of defeat", and all that. It's the culmination of months of effort, travel, quadrants, the ever-weakening bubble, the miraculous mid-major. It's awesome.


As such, I've gathered resources over the years, both to help me understand a game I never played and to win cold hard cash from my coworkers. I've tried to split these into categories for easier consumption.
